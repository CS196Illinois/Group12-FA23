The intention of the project is to use machine learning concepts to predict the outcomes of games between 1950 to 2024 even if that specific matchup was not actually played in the past.

To do this, we used historical data of the outcomes of games and our algorithm uses the teams performance from past games up to 5 years ago and if they have a field advantage.
